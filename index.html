SPACE WAR!

A recreation of an og game but with a bit more stuff.. :)

  • Use w,a,s,d or ↑, ↓, →, ← for movements
  • Click to shoot projectiles
  • You get points based on the distance from which you shoot an alien
  • Every 30 seconds, the game level increases and you get some points!
